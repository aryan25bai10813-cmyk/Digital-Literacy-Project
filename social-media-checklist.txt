SOCIAL MEDIA DO’S AND DON’TS (FOR COLLEGE STUDENTS)

DO’s:
1. Use respectful and professional language in posts and comments.
2. Think before posting anything online.
3. Protect your personal information and privacy.
4. Verify information before sharing it.
5. Use social media for learning, networking, and skill development.

DON’Ts:
1. Do not share offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content.
2. Do not post personal or sensitive information publicly.
3. Do not engage in online arguments or conflicts.
4. Do not spread fake news or unverified information.
5. Do not waste excessive time scrolling without purpose.
