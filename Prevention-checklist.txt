Prevention CheckList

1. Always verify links before clicking, especially in emails or messages.
2. Never share OTPs, passwords, or personal details with anyone online.
3. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on all important accounts.
4. Use strong and unique passwords for different websites.
5. Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or sensitive transactions.
6. Never approve unknown payment requests on UPI apps (like GPay, PhonePe).
7. Double-check UPI IDs and names before sending money.
8. Do not scan unknown QR codes, as they may lead to fraud.
9. Report cyber fraud immediately at https://cybercrime.gov.in
10. Call helpline number 1930 for urgent financial fraud complaints.
